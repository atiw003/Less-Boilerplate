Less Boilerplate Demo Page

this header section (and each section below) uses the .wrapOut and .wrapIn classes
I went through my recent design projects and pulled out the common styles among them. Here they are for your design pleasure!

Written in Less.CSS.
Here's a rounded div with a nice shadow...

.borderRadius(15px);
.boxShadow(2px, 2px, 10px, #000);
...another with a gradient...

.gradientV(@lavender, @red);
...and a gradient going the other way.

.gradientH(@lavender, @red);
They're stacked on top of each other using the .floatClear class
This is variable width, but always centered. Hover here to try it!    See? Still centered.
